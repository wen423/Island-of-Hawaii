Welcome to the Island of Hawaii

The island of Hawaii is the youngest and largest island in the Hawaiian chain. Here you can learn everything about the activities that Hawaii offers to its visitors.

The island is often referred to as the Island of Hawaii or Hawaii Island to distinguish it from the state. It is also referred to as The Big Island, due to its size relative to the other islands.

Activities

The island of Hawaii offers a variety of places to explore, including some of Hawaii's most incredible natural wonders and different water activities. Here are some examples:

  • Surfing
  • Fishing
  • Scuba Diving
